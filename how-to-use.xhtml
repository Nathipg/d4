HOW TO USE

Now that you had discovery this new application, you need to know what you have to do to start to use it. This tutorial is going to be made in two versions, desktop and mobile.

Mobile installation
  1. Access the app center of your smartphone (if you use Android, you should access Play Store, if you use iOS, App Store and if you use Windows Phone, Store);
  2. Type “Evernote” in the search;
  3. Select the application;
  4. Click on “Install”;
  5. Wait until the process finish;
  6. Click on the icon.
Desktop installation
  1. Access the website: https://evernote.com/
  2. Click on the menu icon at the top right corner;
  3. Select the option “Download Evernote”;
  4. Choose your platform and download it.
Create a text note
  1. Click on the plus “+” icon on bottom right;
  2. Click on the "text" icon;
  3. Fill the title and the note fields;
  4. Choose the notebook where you want to store it;
  5. Click the check icon on top left corner to finish.
Create a camera note
  1. Click on the plus “+” icon on bottom right;
  2. Click on the "camera" icon;
  3. Align the camera with a document, blackboard, bulletin board, anything that you want to transform into a note;
  4. Wait while Evernote capturing the photo, and process the better adjust;
  5. Choose a type when you desire in combo box;
  6. Click on “save” on top right.
Create an attachment note
  1. Click on plus “+” icon on bottom right;
  2. Click on “Attachment” icon;
  3. Choose the type of attachment that you want;
  4. Navigate to file’s path;
  5. Select the file;
  6. Click the check icon on top left corner to finish.
Create an audio note
  1. Click on plus “+” icon on bottom right;
  2. Click on “Audio” icon;
  3. Record the audio;
  4. Click on “Stop” icon on top left;
  5. Click the “Check” icon on top left to finish.
Create a reminder note
To create a reminder, you should:
  1. Click on plus “+” icon on bottom right;
  2. Click on “Reminder” icon;
  3. Select the notebook to be remembered;
  4. Set the date of the reminder;
  5. Click on “Define Reminder.

Other way is remember the notes. In one note, do:
  1. Click on the alarm clock then choose date;
  2. Evernote will alert you of upcoming Reminders with in-app notifications, emails, and a badge on the app tile;
  3. Once you’ve set a Reminder for a note, it will appear at the top of your note list, and you can check it off once it’s no longer a priority or has been accomplished.
Create a writing note
  1. Click on plus “+” icon on bottom right;
  2. Click on "Pen(writing)" icon;
  3. In this function you need only select one tool in the top bar (eraser, selection or change colors and dot size);
  4. Slide your finger or smartpen on the screen;
  5. When you finish click on back(arrow in the top left);
  6. Now you can add photos, text, attachments and share;
  7. After, click on confirm on top left to save.
